Line Length

The number of characters per line influences readability. Studies indicate the following guidelines for English:

  • Satisfactory 45-75 characters (desktop) or 35-40 characters (mobile) per line is widely regarded as a satisfactory line length.
  • Optimal Around 66 characters (desktop) or 39 characters (mobile) is optimal.
  • Too long More than 90 characters (desktop) or 50 characters (mobile) is likely too long for continuous reading.
